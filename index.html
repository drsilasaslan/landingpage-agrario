What is app-agrario?

app-agrario is a fast-paced browser game inspired by classic .io titles. Control your circular avatar, collect food, and outmaneuver both players and NPCs to become the largest on the map!

  • Real-time multiplayer action
  • Custom circular avatars
  • Simple, intuitive mouse controls
  • HTML5 canvas graphics & WebSockets

Multiplayer

Compete with players from around the world in real time.

Custom Avatars

Play as a unique circular avatar – upload your own image!

Simple Controls

Move your mouse to control your avatar and collect food dots.

How to Play

  1. Move your mouse to direct your avatar.
  2. Collect blue food dots to grow larger.
  3. Avoid bigger players and NPCs – they can eat you!
  4. Try to outmaneuver and consume smaller players.
  5. Press r to respawn if needed.
